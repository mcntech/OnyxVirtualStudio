Using Onyx you can compose a 3D scene and map the video feeds from Skype video call, webcam, IP Cam as well as WMV and MP4 files. The 3D scene can be navigated mimicking the motion of a video camera. You can create very effective  collaborations/presentations and can also record the session.

<h1>Creates a Virtual Meeting Place</h1>
<h2>Onyx can merge your web cam video feed and the remote participant's video stream in a 3D scene and creates a virtual meeting place.</h2> 
<h1>Effective Video Presentations</h1>
<h2>You can bring your PowerPoint slide shows, other presentation videos and IP Cam feeds into the Onyx Virtual Studio and present to the participant.</h2> 
<h1>3D Navigation similar to a TV studio setting</h1>
<h2>Navigate to any part of the scene showing details of your presentation or change the focus to speaker or participant similar to a TV studio setting. </h2> 
<h1><strong>Recording</h1>
<h2>Record the presentation session in MP4 or HTTP Live Streaming formats.</h2>
